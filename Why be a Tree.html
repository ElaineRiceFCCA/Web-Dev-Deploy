Why be a Tree?

The chief advantage in being a tree is the amount of light it gets simply by being bigger than other plants. The disadvantage of being a tree is that it must survive in one place for a long time. If fires, landslides, or hurricanes are regular events, the tree may die without ever having reproduced. In fact, human activity is the major factor militating against them. For example, it is thought that about 10,000 years ago, 80% of Ireland was covered by woodland, while today it is only 11%.
